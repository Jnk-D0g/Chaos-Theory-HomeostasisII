Summary of bill SJ255:

Essentially this bill is proposing that a study be conducted in order to identify issues found within the campaigning of politicians by November 30, 2026. Which mostly include financing and election laws .There will also be a budget proposed to implement changes when their data is presented to the general assembly in 2027.The main stakeholders of this bill would be the potential donors of political campaigns and all people involved on that end.
