Stake-Holders

Stakeholder Description
J.L.A.R.C The entity that would be conducting the investigations into campaigning and implementing changes as necessary.
Campaign staff Directly impacted by the bill because they are the ones who would have their work investigated and studied and they would also have to accept the changes implemented.
Campaigning politicians The rules that the politicians would have to follow when campaigning are set by the J.L.A.R.C.
Donors Political donors have stake in this situation because they are the ones who fund the campaigns.
The common citizen and taxpayers Anyone who can vote or be influenced by political advertisements.
